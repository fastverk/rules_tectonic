\section{The Equation}
\label{sec:body}

A short equation to confirm packages from \texttt{preamble.tex}
propagate through the wrapper:

\begin{equation}
  \zeta(s) \;=\; \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{1}{n^{s}}
\end{equation}

Cross-referencing section~\ref{sec:intro} also works — labels
defined in one section are visible from another because the
wrapper \texttt{\textbackslash{}input}s both before tectonic
runs its second pass.
